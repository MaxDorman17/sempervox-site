Why us

What makes us different.

Managed IT is about more than keeping the lights on. Here's what working with Sempervox actually looks like.

Local & accessible

We're based in Leicester city centre. You deal with the same team who knows your setup — not a rotating helpdesk that needs briefing from scratch every time you call.

Proactive, not reactive

We monitor your infrastructure and act before things break. Managed IT means prevention first — not just a fast response after something's already cost you downtime.

About Sempervox

A technology partner with a long-term view.

Sempervox (trading as Signum Corporate Communications) has been supporting businesses across the East Midlands for over two decades.
