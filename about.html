About

Two decades of managed IT in the East Midlands.

Sempervox has been supporting businesses with their technology since the early 2000s. We're not a startup with a pitch deck — we're a team with a track record.

Who we are

Your IT partner, not just your IT supplier.

Sempervox trades as Signum Corporate Communications and is based in Leicester city centre. We've been operating as a managed service provider for over 20 years, working with businesses across the East Midlands that need their IT to be reliable, secure, and managed by people who understand their goals.

The difference between a managed IT partner and a break-fix IT company is significant. A break-fix company turns up when something goes wrong. We take responsibility for your technology infrastructure proactively — monitoring, maintaining, updating, and improving it so that problems are caught before they become incidents, and incidents are resolved before they become disasters.

We're a Microsoft Silver Partner, Fortinet specialists, Cyber Essentials certified, and work with a range of technology partners to ensure we're recommending the right solutions — not just the ones we happen to sell.

We're accountable for outcomes When we manage your IT, we own the result. Not the ticket count, not the hours logged — the actual outcome for your business.
We give honest advice If the answer to your problem is something simple and cheap, we'll tell you that. We're not looking for the most expensive solution.
We keep things simple Technology can be complex. Explaining it, and the work we do, shouldn't be. We talk plainly and make sure you always know what's going on.
We think long-term We're not interested in quick wins that create problems later. Technology decisions have consequences, and we plan for them.
