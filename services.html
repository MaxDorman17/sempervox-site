Services

Managed IT across six service areas.

We don't do one thing well and bolt on the rest. Each of these services is core to what we do — supported by the right technology partnerships and experienced people.

Service 01
Digital Transformation

Digital transformation isn't a project with an end date — it's a continuous process of making your technology work harder for your business. We assess your current IT landscape, identify what's holding you back, and build a practical roadmap that aligns technology investment with your actual business goals.

Whether that means migrating to Microsoft 365, moving infrastructure to Azure, automating manual processes, or retiring legacy systems that cost more to maintain than they're worth — we plan it, deliver it, and support the outcome.

We don't sell technology for its own sake. Every recommendation is grounded in a genuine understanding of how your organisation operates and what would meaningfully improve it.

Microsoft 365 Azure Cloud migration Process automation Legacy modernisation
Service 02
Security

The threat landscape has changed significantly. Ransomware, phishing, and supply chain attacks are no longer abstract risks — they affect businesses of every size, including those in the East Midlands. The question isn't whether you're a target; it's whether you're prepared.

We take a layered approach: Cyber Essentials certification as a baseline, Fortinet-powered perimeter security, endpoint protection, multi-factor authentication, and staff awareness training that actually sticks. We also carry out vulnerability assessments and produce reports that give you and your board a clear picture of your current exposure.

Security doesn't have to mean complexity. Our goal is protection that works without slowing your team down.

Fortinet Cyber Essentials Endpoint protection MFA Vulnerability assessment
Service 03
Networks

Network infrastructure is the foundation everything else sits on. When it works well, nobody notices. When it doesn't, everything stops. We design networks with performance and reliability as the primary requirement — not just whatever is cheapest or quickest to install.

We work with Ubiquiti for enterprise-grade wireless deployments and DrayTek for robust routing and WAN connectivity. We design and install structured cabling, configure VLANs for traffic segmentation, deploy VPN solutions for remote access, and manage ongoing network monitoring and maintenance.

Whether you're a single-site business or running multiple locations, we engineer the network around your requirements and manage it as part of your overall IT service.

Ubiquiti DrayTek LAN / WLAN VLAN VPN SD-WAN
Service 04
Mobility

The expectation that work only happens at a fixed desk in a fixed office is long gone. Your team needs to be productive on any device, from any location, without your IT team losing visibility or control of what's connected to your systems.

We source and manage mobile devices and data plans, configure mobile device management (MDM) policies that enforce security standards without making devices difficult to use, and set up secure remote access so staff working from home or on the road have the same experience as being in the office.

BYOD policies, corporate device management, mobile airtime — we handle all of it as part of a joined-up service.

MDM BYOD policy Mobile airtime Remote access Device provisioning
Service 05
Unified Communications

Most businesses use too many separate tools to communicate — a phone system, a video platform, a messaging app, and an email client that don't talk to each other. Unified communications brings all of that into one consistent experience.

We implement and support Microsoft Teams Voice, hosted telephony through Gamma, and other UC platforms depending on your requirements. That includes number porting, desk phone hardware, auto-attendants, call queues, and call recording where required.

We also manage ongoing changes — new users, number changes, DDI management — as part of your service, so you're not chasing a telecoms provider every time something needs updating.

Microsoft Teams Voice Gamma Hosted telephony Number porting Call recording
Service 06
Resilient Connectivity

For most businesses, internet connectivity is now as fundamental as electricity. A single point of failure is simply not acceptable — yet many businesses are running on a single consumer-grade broadband connection with no failover plan.

We source and manage business-grade connectivity including leased lines with guaranteed speeds and SLAs, bonded broadband, and 4G/5G mobile failover that automatically activates if your primary connection goes down. For multi-site businesses, we also design and manage WAN connectivity that links your locations reliably and securely.

Connectivity is specified, installed, and monitored by us — not handed off to a carrier with a phone number and a warranty document.

Leased lines Dual ISP 4G / 5G failover Bonded broadband WAN
