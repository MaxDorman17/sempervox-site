How we handle support

What happens when you contact us.

Every support request is logged and tracked. Whether you call, email, or use the portal, you'll always have a ticket reference and a named engineer handling your case.

We triage requests by severity — critical issues affecting your whole business are responded to within the hour. For less urgent issues, we'll acknowledge within one business hour and resolve within the timeframe agreed in your service contract.

If you're not an existing Sempervox client and you have an IT emergency, call us anyway. We'll do our best to help.

Every issue is tracked You'll always have a ticket number. No requests disappear into a phone call with nothing to show for it.
Named engineers You'll know who is working on your issue. No anonymous queues, no being bounced between people.
You're kept informed We update you as work progresses. You shouldn't have to chase us for a status update.
Root cause, not just fixes We fix the immediate issue and tell you why it happened — so you can decide whether to address the underlying cause.
