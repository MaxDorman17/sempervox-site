About Sempervox

A technology partner with a long-term view.

Sempervox (trading as Signum Corporate Communications) has been supporting businesses across the East Midlands for over two decades. We're a managed service provider — which means we take responsibility for your IT infrastructure and keep it running, rather than just turning up when something breaks.

We work with businesses that value clear communication, consistent service, and an IT partner who understands their goals. Our team holds experience across the full stack of technologies we recommend and support.
